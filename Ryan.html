Ryan Wu

Co-Founder / Co-President

Ryan Wu has been passionate about table tennis from a young age. He has dedicated his life to mastering the game and sharing his skills with others. As a co-founder and co-president, Ryan has helped shape our organization’s mission and vision. With his years of experience, he brings invaluable insights to the team.

Ryan is not only an exceptional player but also an inspiring mentor. His leadership and passion are evident in everything he does. Ryan’s goal is to make table tennis accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of age or skill level.
