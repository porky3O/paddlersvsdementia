"Meet Us: The Visionary High Schoolers Behind Paddles Against Dementia

As we step onto the path of driving change, we, a group of passionate high school students, proudly introduce ourselves as the driving force behind Paddlers Against Dementia. Our journey began with a shared dedication to decreasing the chance of developing dementia in the elderly, a cause that ignited a spark within each of us. Ryan Wu, Jensen Feng, Victor Xie, and Raymond Zhu are our names, and our collective commitment to creating a positive impact knows no bounds. With an unwavering belief in our capacity to effect change, we've defied conventional expectations and harnessed our youthful energy to establish Paddlers Against Dementia—a testament to the strength of youthful determination and a shared vision for a better world."

Our Mission

Empowering Minds Through Ping Pong: Our mission at 'Paddlers Against Dementia' is to enhance the well-being of seniors by introducing them to the joy of ping pong. By fostering an inclusive and active community centered around this engaging sport, we aim to reduce the risk of dementia among the elderly population. Through friendly competition, social interaction, and cognitive stimulation, we aspire to strengthen cognitive resilience, promote mental agility, and create lasting connections that contribute to a vibrant and dementia-resistant future for our senior participants.

About Us

Ryan Wu (Co-Founder/President)

Hello, I'm Ryan Wu, and table tennis has been a defining passion in my life since a very young age. I've had the incredible privilege of representing the Under 17 National Team, and my journey through the sport has been nothing short of amazing. I've been fortunate to win numerous awards and accolades that have fueled my love for table tennis.


Jensen Feng (Co-Founder/President)

I'm Jensen Feng, and my journey in table tennis has been an incredible ride. I'm currently on both the Under 17 and Under 19 National Teams, and it's been a privilege to represent my country in this sport for several consecutive years. As I continue my journey in this sport, I'm excited to see what the future holds and am committed to pushing my limits to achieve even greater heights.


Victor Xie (Coach)

I'm Victor Xie, a 15-year-old with a passion for table tennis that's been burning bright since I was a kid. Currently, I'm proud to be a part of the Under 17 National Team, representing my country in a sport that I love. My journey in table tennis has been marked by countless hours of practice and dedication. I'm no stranger to competition, having clinched victory in numerous tournaments during my earlier years. But what drives me is the constant pursuit of improvement. Every day, I step onto the table with the goal of getting better, refining my skills, and pushing my limits.


Raymond Zhu (Coach)

I'm Raymond Zhu, a 16-year-old with a burning passion for table tennis. While I haven't yet had the chance to represent my country on the national stage, I believe that my relentless work ethic will propel me to the Under 17 National Team next year. My journey in table tennis has been defined by determination and perseverance. I've poured countless hours into practice, honing my skills and sharpening my game. The dream of wearing the national colors is what keeps me going, and I'm determined to make it a reality.
